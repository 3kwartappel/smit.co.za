So I created an online service called LyricsMeaning to do just that, and also the functionality to add comments/interpretation of the lyrics.

All you need is winamp (5.5 or greater) installed, then you can add the online service directly from here.

Whenever a song is played, it will automatically search the web for the lyrics and display it. Â If it cannot be found you can add the lyrics yourself. Â  Interpretation / comments are open to all, and it’s nice to see other’s interpretation of the lyrics.
