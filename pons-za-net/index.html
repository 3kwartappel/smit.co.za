Pons.za.net

I am a real fan of bash.org, it contains lots of funny quotes from MIRC channels. But there was no equivalent site for the Afrikaans language (or something specific to South Africa). That’s why I created pons.za.net

Users submit funny Afrikaans quotes, and then its up or down-voted by the public.
